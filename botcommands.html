Old School RuneScape

Old School RuneScape (OSRS) is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by Jagex. The game was first released in 22 February 2013. When Old School RuneScape launched, it was originally an August 2007 build of RuneScape. However, it has since received engine improvements, new content, and quality of life updates largely decided by in-game polls. Despite having a smaller development team and a slower relative update schedule, Old School RuneScape has a larger player-base than RuneScape.

Features

Here are some of the features of Old School RuneScape:

  • Community-led development: frequent updates, voted on by players
  • Fight legendary bosses
  • Explore a vast world
  • Play alone or with friends
  • Experience complex and satisfying combat mechanics
