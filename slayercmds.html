Slayer Bot Commands

All the slayer commands and info.

-task

Assigns you a slayer task which you can then complete to earn slayer xp, gold and items
you can complete slayer tasks by typing the -slay.
This command has a daily limit that increases as your level increases:
1-15 (5 Tasks)
15-30 (7 Tasks)
30-45 (10 Tasks)
45-65 (10 Tasks)
65-80 (13 Tasks)
80-99 (16 Tasks)
99+ (20 Tasks).

-slay

Completes your slayer task that you where asigned from using the -task command

-tasks

Shows you a complete log of all the slayer tasks you are able to be given by each slayer master with the slayer masters names at the top.
(Npc Name and required level)

-reset

If you have a TimeCrystal in your inventory you are able t reset your daily tasks but typing -reset.
(can be found from completing sayer tasks and by opening the crystal chest)

Slayer Scrolls

Slayer Scrolls are obtained by completing slayer tasks and from the crystal chest
if you have a slayer scroll in your inventory your next -task will not count towards your daily slayer tasks.
